FOOTBALL

- Maximum 15 players per team.

- Duration of each half will be 35 minutes.

- The break between two halves will be of 10 minutes.

- In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the officials and the LAC committee would be final and abiding.

Entry Fees per Player - 500(with Accomodation & Food)/ 200(without Accomodation & Food)

CHESS

- Maximum 5 players per team.

- Duration of each match will be 45 minutes.

- Rules will be declared in the event.

- In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the officials and the LAC committee would be final and abiding.

Entry Fees per Player - 500(with Accomodation & Food)/ 300(without Accomodation & Food)

TABLE TENNIS

- Maximum 5 players(4 for girls) per team.

- Each match will be played best-of-5-sets(or 3 if required) basis.

Format:

1st match: 1st singles

2nd match: 2nd singles

3rd match: 1st doubles

4th match: 3rd singles

5th match: 2st singles (If required)

Entry Fees per Player - 500(with Accomodation & Food)/ 200(without Accomodation & Food)

LAWN TENNIS

- 3 to 5 players per team.

- Format will be Davis Cup style. Singles, doubles & singles.

- Single will be best of 15 games and doubles will be of best of 19 games.

- Only one player can play both singles and doubles.

- In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the officials and the LAC committee would be final and abiding.

Entry Fees per Player - 500(with Accomodation & Food)/ 300(without Accomodation & Food)

VOLLEYBALL

- Maximum 12 players per team.

- Each match will be played best-of-5-sets(or 3 if required) basis.

- In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the officials and the LAC committee would be final and abiding.

Entry Fees per Player - 500(with Accomodation & Food)/ 300(without Accomodation & Food)
